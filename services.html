Loans

Types of Loans

We offer differEnt types of loans, this includes:

  • Normal Loans
  • Top Up Loans
  • Emergency Loans
  • School fees loans
  • Premium Loans
  • Mega/ Consolidated Loans

Requirements and Features

School Fees Loan

A school fees loan is designed to assist a member pay school fees in various institutions of learning. It could be colleges, Universities or Seconday schools.This loan is for you!

Emergency Loan

An Emergency loan is designed to help a member meet an unexpected short-term or personal need such as medical bills, funeral expenses, court fines, other unexpected calamities and other personal needs. This loan is for you!

Normal Loans

A normal loan is designed for development purposes. It enables you purchase or build a house, purchase a plot, buy a car, start a business or even do large-scale farming.

Requirements And Features:

  • Be a Sacco member with consistent contributions for a minimum of 6 months. Defaulters don’t qualify. Additionally, you will need to provide a collateral to qualify for the loan.
  • You will be eligible for a loan of upto four times (x4) your current total Main Savings Account contributions.
  • The amount borrowed shall not exceed KES 3 Million; however a higher loan amount may be approved subject to availability of funds.
  • The repayment period shall be a maximum of 36 months.
  • You will need to have Sacco escrow account with equivalent of at least three months loan repayment amount. This balance must be maintained throughout the loan duration.
  • The loan shall attract an interest rate of 1.00% per month on a reducing balance.
  • Pay a non-refundable loan-processing fee of 1% of loan amount subject to a minimum of $100.
  • Show proof of ability to repay the loan – paystubs, other incomes etc.
  • The Security shall be the member’s savings or other acceptable property. No member guarantors accepted.
  • If you use a developed property (e.g. a house) as collateral, the loan value will not exceed 80% of the market value of the property used as a collateral.
  • If you use an undeveloped property (e.g. a plot) as collateral, the loan given will not exceed 75% of market value of the undeveloped property used as a collateral.

Documents Required when Applying loans:

If you are employed:

  • Pay-stubs for the latest 3 months (Notarized)
  • Letter from employer confirming employment status (Notarized)
  • Bank statements for the latest 6 months (Notarized)
  • Copy of Kenya ID or Kenya valid Passport
  • KRA PIN Certificate
  • Copy of the latest W2 Form issued by the employer
  • Security - Copy of the title document of a property acceptable by the Sacco (Minimum lease term 35 years)

If you are Self-employed or Contract:

  • Bank statements for the latest 12 months (Notarized)
  • Copy of Kenya ID or Kenya valid Passport
  • KRA PIN certificate
  • Business registration certificate
  • IRS Business Tax Returns for the latest filing year
  • Security - Copy of the title document of a property acceptable by the Sacco (Minimum lease term 35 years)
